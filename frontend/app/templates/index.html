Welcome Incoming Exchange Students!

Explore Venice with Ca' Foscari University

Group Composition

Marta, Francesco, Carlo, Andrea, and Antonio, we are five students of the second year of digital management. We know what you might be thinking... Why are you doing it? The reasons are many; some of us will go on Erasmus and find difficulty in converting it, while others just need to anticipate some exams to finish earlier. We are all somehow forerunning the time!

Stress-Free Erasmus: It's Possible!

We have decided to develop this project since almost all of us have experienced the stress of checking and scheduling the exams to convalidate during the Exchange semester. Therefore, we want to prevent anyone else from experiencing it.

Purpose

We wanted to provide Ca’ Foscari with an efficient solution that may reduce the friction and somehow increase the willingness of students to come and study in Venice, granting an intrinsic benefit for our community and the overall prestige of our University.

Planning Your Erasmus Exchange Got Easier!

Our exchange student calendar will provide the user with a search bar with two preliminary filters:

  1. Choose among the different locations Ca’ Foscari provides (Venice, Mestre, Roncade, Treviso).
  2. Choose among the academic path: Bachelor/Master.
  3. Search for a course.
