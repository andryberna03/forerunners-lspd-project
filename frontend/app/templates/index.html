Welcome Exchange Students!

Stress-Free Exchange: It's Possible!

We've decided to embark on this project because many of us have firsthand experience with the stress of organizing and scheduling exams to earn credits during exchange semesters. Our aim is to spare others from going through the same ordeal.

OUR MISSION

Streamline the retrieval of information about Ca' Foscari's teachings to attract more exchange students to Venice, enriching our community and aiding our university in enhancing its internationalization efforts.

Ca'Foscari University

Ca' Foscari University of Venice, established on August 6, 1868, is a renowned Italian public university. It enjoys both national and international recognition for its exceptional research endeavors and the excellence of its teaching methods, characterized by an international and interdisciplinary approach. Situated in the heart of a truly remarkable city, the university fosters a diverse and global community of scholars, students, and researchers.
