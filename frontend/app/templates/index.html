Our Mission

Simplify Ca' Foscari admissions to attract more students to Venice, enriching our community and university.

Ca'Foscari University

The Ca' Foscari University of Venice is an Italian public university founded on August 6, 1868. It is nationally and internationally recognized for its research achievements and the quality of its teaching, which have an international and interdisciplinary approach. It attracts and supports a diverse and international community of individuals who study, teach, and conduct research in the heart of a uniquely remarkable city.
